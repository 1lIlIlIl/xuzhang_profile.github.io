Neuromorphic Computing

Wearable Integration

Integrated wearable systems for multimodal sensing, emotion monitoring, and real-time health tracking. Flexible circuit design and efficient signal processing for continuous physiological monitoring applications.

Photodetector & Light Emitting Diodes

Advanced optoelectronic devices with flexible substrates for sensing and light emission. Design and fabrication of integrated photodetector-LED systems for optical applications and wearable spectroscopy.

Self-Powered Wearable Recognizer for Multimodal Emotion Monitoring

June 2024 — Present

A fully integrated, self-powered multi-modal emotion-recognizer-on-patch (SP-MER) system integrating flexible circuit with 8-channel EEG array and digital microphone, powered by organic photovoltaic module. Machine learning algorithm autonomously selects optimal EEG channel for data quality assurance. Cloud-based fusion model interprets multimodal data stream achieving over 92.85% accuracy for continuous emotion classification, enabling robust monitoring for mental health and human-computer interaction applications.
